Who We Are?

Ornate TechnoServices is an accredited organization by Microsoft, ISO and CMMI level 3. The company began its operations in 2009 as an offshore outsourcing proprietorship and in 2011 it became a Private limited company.

Our Vision is to Innovate, focus on Growth & Expansion, be a customer centric & Focused team where we aim to build strong relationships with customers, earn their loyalty, and provide exceptional experiences.
